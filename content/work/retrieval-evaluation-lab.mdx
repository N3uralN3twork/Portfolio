Retrieval systems become difficult to reason about when every change is measured on a different slice of data. This lab keeps corpora, chunking, embeddings, rerankers, and prompts versioned together.

<Callout title="Design bias">
Optimize for comparisons that can be repeated, not one-off leaderboards that look decisive and then evaporate.
</Callout>

The scoring function combines relevance and latency:

$$
J = nDCG@10 - 0.08 \cdot \log(1 + p95)
$$

```python
def rank_run(run):
    return run.ndcg_at_10 - 0.08 * math.log1p(run.latency_p95_ms)
```
